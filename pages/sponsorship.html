What You Can Do For Us

Sponsored Materials

Running a club of this size can be expensive, especially with all the material requiered for maintaining software.

Any kind of sponsored materials, such as shirts, prizes for competitions, or even just a donation can go a very long way.

We accept all kinds of donations towards our cause.

Event Sponsorship

We host competitions and hope to go to conferences to spread our message.

These are costly activities that requires a large time commitment for everyone involved.

Your help in sponsoring these events would allow us to not only have a higher student engagement, but provide real incentive for students to compete by learning new technologies.

Guest Speakers

We want our students to know what the real world is like.

We are always looking for people to share their stories and have their voices heard regarding the software industry and both the struggle and success in it.

This is an amazing opportunity for student outreach and gives everyone involved the ability to get to know and understand the software world better.
