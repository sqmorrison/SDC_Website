About Us

Who We Are

The Software Development Club aims to fix the common problem around campus of low representation for those who want to program.

We reach several majors, including Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, and Business IT.

Our goal is to improve campus through student led projects designed to revitalize campus and provide utility to both students and faculty alike.

We will also be hosting other events to cultivate student success, such as supplementary lectures and new technology competitions.

We are the solution to the problems presented by the timeline of various degrees.
